BENGALURU, Where Tradition Meets Innovation


Bengaluru, the capital of Karnataka, harmoniously blends its historical legacy with cutting-edge technology. Known as the “Silicon Valley of India,” this vibrant city has witnessed groundbreaking scientific achievements, including Nobel laureate C.V. Raman’s work on light scattering. Additionally, Bengaluru boasts engineering excellence, with figures like Sir Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya leaving an indelible mark. Notably, central government organizations and PSUs, such as DRDO, ISRO, BEL, and BEML, contribute to its dynamic landscape. Whether you’re a tech enthusiast, a food lover, or a culture seeker, Bengaluru invites you to explore its vibrant streets, savour local delicacies, and embrace the perfect blend of tradition and innovation.

Join us at the workshop and experience Bengaluru’s magic firsthand!
